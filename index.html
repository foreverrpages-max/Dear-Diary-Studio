Process

A small studio,
one page at a time.

We're not a generator. There is no template. Every page is a tiny commission — words written for them, photos arranged carefully, music chosen with you. Here's the rhythm of how it goes.

i.

Tell us about them

The occasion. Their name, your inside jokes, the things only you'd know.

ii.

We write & design

Letters, poems, captions — written by our team. Photos placed and edited.

iii.

You shape it

Up to four rounds of changes. A word, a photo, a whole section — your call.

iv.

A link, just for them

One quiet URL. Send it whenever feels right. They open. Time stops.
